NOBLE PARTNER 2018, FIELD SANITATIONAND CLIMATE CONTROL

Vaziani military base, Georgia

Jul 21, 2018 — Aug 17, 2018

US Army Corps

Noble Partner 2018 is a Georgian Armed Forces and U.S. Army Europe cooperatively-led exercise in it's fourth iteration. For this year's exercise our company delivered and serviced 150 portable chemical latrines, 40 portable sinks, 6 shower containers and 6 industrial air-control units. For the operation of the shower facilities we build an ad-hoc water distribution and collection system with our own power supply. We also provided 24/7 electrical power for the ACs. With a crew of 30 people, both local nationals and our company representatives, we provided daily services for the entire duration of the exercise covering an area of 10 000 hectares.

For more information on the Noble Partner 2018 exercise, please visit: http://www.eur.army.mil/NoblePartner/
